Hysterically fun and cheap, too!
Roku streams content from the internet to your TV.  It has somewhere around 750 channels, many of them free, some, like Netflix, for a monthly fee.  It can connect via ethernet cable to your router or wirelessly if your router is so equipped.  Setup is trivial; plug it into the TV and the wall outlet and it connects and goes to work. WARNING: this gadget is habit forming,  Expect some long nights exploring and enjoying the offerings.
