Straight out of the box, I put cold, sudsy water into the carafe and swirled it lightly with my hand-- didn't even think I was touching the inside-- and all of the sudden, "POP!" Everyone in the room looked up, startled, and I pulled my hand out of the carafe, covered in glass shards and bleeding! I was not scrubbing as the thing had never been used-- it was FREAKY!
