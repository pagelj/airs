Everything Worked
Product on time, was as described, and worked!  It even came with a tool kit to complete the install unlike some of the other comments.  The only thing that I wish I caught before I completed the install was that the ear speaker protective screen is not included with the new screen. Remove it from your old one and put it on the new one before you start the reassembly. I printed out a sample diagram of the phone from [...] and rolled little pieces of tape and placed them on the diagram where the screws went and placed the screws on the tape as I went.  This helped out big time so I didn't loose any screws and made it easy to see where they needed to be reinstalled. I rubbed my screw drivers on a magnet so the screws were easier to work with.
