Billionaire Wins!
I truly in enjoyed the first book in the BIllionaire Bachelor series! Joseph Anderson wants to see his son settle down and start a family. All the son want to remain Bachelors. Joseph had some work to do.  He decide to start with his son Lucas who is currently running the family business since Joseph retired.  Joseph has found the perfect companion Amy.  Amy is a sweet young woman who is determine to better her life.  Joseph has deicde Amy is the woman for Lucas. Lucas have instant attraction they both try to fight! I love watching the couple interaction.  This a wonderful story about finding that special someone, family and romance! A great read!
