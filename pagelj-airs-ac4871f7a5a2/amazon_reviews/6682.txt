Very happy with this screen protector
The package comes with good instructions and links to youtube videos to help you apply the screen protector.  Also it comes with a cleaning cloth and sticky tab for removing lint.  This is a clear screen protector, so after applying it on my phone I cannot even tell its there.  I have also used one of the anti glare protectors on my other phone and have been also very pleased. The protective film on both sides of the screen protector are very helpful in the application process.
