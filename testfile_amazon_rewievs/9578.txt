Iphone 5 and Pioneer AVH-X1500
This unit is absolutely a great product and you might as well use it as it was intended. When I first purchased my Iphone 5 I was not able to use the original cable provided as an audio output. After a lot of research I found the following solution below. Solution to the dilemma with the Iphone 5 and this unit which only worked with the Iphone 4/4S. All you need it to purchase is Apple Lightning to 30-Pin Adapter (0.2M) this little cable will resolve the issue with not being able to use the phone the same as the 4/4S. Apple Lightning to 30-Pin Adapter (0.2M)
